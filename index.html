A Story Steeped in Passion

From humble beginnings to a beloved community hub, our journey is one of dedication to the craft of coffee.

Crafting Community Through Coffee

Founded in 2015 by coffee enthusiasts Elena Rodriguez and James Wilson, Brewed Awakening began as a small roastery in Portland's historic Pearl District. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved community gathering place, known for its exceptional coffee and welcoming atmosphere.

Our mission is simple: to source the world's finest beans ethically, roast them to perfection in-house, and serve them with genuine hospitality. We believe coffee is more than a beverage—it's a catalyst for connection, creativity, and community.

"Coffee is the common thread that connects us all—farmers, roasters, baristas, and drinkers. At Brewed Awakening, we honor this connection with every cup we serve."

Why Brewed Awakening?

From Bean to Cup

Our quest for the perfect coffee takes us around the world. We partner with small, dedicated farms in the most renowned coffee-growing regions.

Antigua, Guatemala

Nestled between three volcanoes, the rich soil produces beans with a full body, and notes of cocoa and soft spice.

Altitude1,500-1,700m
VarietyBourbon
HarvestNov - Mar

Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia

The birthplace of coffee, Yirgacheffe's beans are renowned for their bright, floral, and citrus notes.

Altitude1,700-2,200m
VarietyHeirloom
HarvestOct - Dec

Sumatra, Indonesia

Known for the unique wet-hulling process, these beans offer a heavy body with deep, earthy flavors of cedar and tobacco.

Altitude1,100-1,300m
VarietyTypica
HarvestSep - Dec
